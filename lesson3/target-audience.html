The target audience

The audience of this site are people who live in the region or come for some reason to these cities and need to know how will be the weather that day.

Personas

The fiance

The bride wants to have her wedding outdoors but needs the forecast to know if it will be possible.

The skier

The skier wants to know if there will be enough snow to be able to ski the next day.

Scenarios

  • It will rain today? Can I go to the beach?
  • Do I need to wake up earlier to take of the snow in my car?
  • Do I need a indoor place for my event tomorrow?
  • Should I take a coat for my travel?
  • Is the sun too strong for my baby?
