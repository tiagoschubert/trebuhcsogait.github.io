Welcome to Cozumel

Around Cozumel

Most of the tourists who spend the day in Cozumel rent a car, jeep or motorbike to go around the island (blue route on the map below, traveled counterclockwise). Along this route are most of Cozumel's main attractions.

South of Cozumel

The south is the best part of Cozumel, where the most structured beaches on the island are. There are simple beach bars, others more structured and parks with a beach, water activities and restaurants. Some beaches and bars on this list are not exactly in the south of the island, but I put them here because they are on the way to the south.
