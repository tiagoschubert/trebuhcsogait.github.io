Visual Hierarchy

Canva

www.canva.com

Canva has a well-defined Visual Hierarchy. With the title in bold and additional information in less thick font. There is also an example of repetition of colors between the logo and the button.

White Space and Clean Design

Google

www.google.com

Google is a great example of White Space and Clean Design. Despite being a site with as much information as possible to find the home page is very simple. Only with a centralized search field.

PARC: Contrast

Apple

www.apple.com

Apple has a design very focused on the contrast between white and black on its website.When background is black, the font is white and vice versa
